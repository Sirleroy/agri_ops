# AgriOps — Subscription Agreement (Template)

> **Note:** This is a working template. Have a Nigerian commercial lawyer review it before
> the first signing — particularly clause 3.4 (data processing) and clause 8 (limitation
> of liability). The review cost is small relative to the risk of an unenforceable clause
> with an institutional tenant.

---

```
AGRIOPS TECHNOLOGIES LTD
Software Subscription Agreement

This Agreement is entered into as of [DATE] between:
```

---

## 1. Subscription and Access

1.1 AgriOps grants the Subscriber a non-exclusive, non-transferable right to access and use
the AgriOps platform ("the Service") during the Subscription Term, subject to the terms of
this Agreement.

1.2 The Subscriber's plan tier, user limit, and feature access are as set out in Schedule A
(Subscription Details) attached to this Agreement.

1.3 Access is provided to authorised users only. The Subscriber is responsible for all
activity on accounts created under their organisation.

---

## 2. Payment

2.1 Subscription fees are as set out in Schedule A. Fees are due in advance at the start of
each billing period.

2.2 Payment is made in the currency specified in Schedule A (NGN or USD). NGN payments are
processed via Paystack; USD payments via Stripe.

2.3 If payment is not received within 14 days of the due date, AgriOps reserves the right to
suspend access until the outstanding amount is settled.

2.4 Fees are non-refundable except where AgriOps terminates the Agreement without cause under
clause 7.2.

---

## 3. Data Ownership

3.1 All data entered into the Service by the Subscriber — including farm coordinates, supplier
records, purchase orders, and compliance outputs — remains the sole property of the Subscriber.

3.2 AgriOps does not claim ownership of Subscriber data and will not use it for any purpose
outside of providing the Service.

3.3 Upon termination, AgriOps will make the Subscriber's data available for export in JSON or
CSV format for 30 days, after which it may be permanently deleted.

3.4 AgriOps processes personal data (farmer names, field officer records) on behalf of the
Subscriber as a data processor under Nigeria's NDPA 2023. The Subscriber is the data
controller and is responsible for ensuring lawful collection from data subjects.

---

## 4. Confidentiality

4.1 Each party agrees to keep confidential any non-public information received from the other
party in connection with this Agreement and not to disclose it to third parties without prior
written consent.

4.2 This obligation does not apply to information that is publicly known, independently
developed, or required to be disclosed by law.

---

## 5. Acceptable Use

The Subscriber agrees not to:

- Use the Service for any unlawful purpose
- Attempt to reverse-engineer, copy, or resell the Service
- Upload data that infringes the rights of third parties
- Share login credentials outside of authorised users within their organisation

---

## 6. Service Availability

6.1 AgriOps will use reasonable efforts to maintain Service availability. Planned maintenance
will be communicated in advance where possible.

6.2 AgriOps does not guarantee uninterrupted access and is not liable for downtime caused by
third-party infrastructure, force majeure, or events outside its reasonable control.

---

## 7. Term and Termination

7.1 This Agreement begins on the Start Date in Schedule A and continues for the Initial Term
specified, then renews automatically on a month-to-month basis unless either party gives
30 days' written notice of non-renewal.

7.2 Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately on written notice if the other party
materially breaches the Agreement and fails to remedy the breach within 14 days of notice.

7.3 AgriOps may terminate with 30 days' notice without cause, in which case a pro-rata refund
of prepaid fees will be issued.

---

## 8. Limitation of Liability

8.1 AgriOps' total liability under this Agreement in any 12-month period shall not exceed the
total fees paid by the Subscriber in that period.

8.2 AgriOps is not liable for indirect, consequential, or incidental losses, including lost
revenue or compliance failures resulting from data inaccuracies introduced by the Subscriber.

---

## 9. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. Any disputes shall
be resolved in the courts of Lagos State.
