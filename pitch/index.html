The Regulatory Shift

The EU Deforestation Regulation has changed the rules of market access.

Effective 2025, exporters must prove their commodities are not sourced from deforested land — with documentary evidence at the farm level.

  • Farm geolocation and polygon boundaries required
  • Supply chain traceability from farm to shipment
  • Due diligence statements for every export consignment
  • 5-year record retention for compliance audit

The Moat

Farm mapping is the most defensible dataset in agricultural systems.

Once captured, farm boundaries, crop production data, supplier relationships, and commodity traceability compound into a long-term agricultural intelligence layer.

  • GPS polygon boundaries — precise, reusable, permanent
  • Farmer identity registry linked to each farm plot
  • Historical traceability chain per commodity lot
  • EUDR deforestation reference baseline locked at 2020

Competitors would need years of field operations to replicate this dataset. The data captured today is the infrastructure for every future intelligence product.
