This Cookie Policy explains how AgriOps Technologies Limited ("AgriOps") uses cookies and similar technologies on the AgriOps platform ("Platform") at app.agriops.io.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember information about your visit — for example, keeping you logged in between page loads.

2. Cookies We Use

AgriOps uses a minimal set of cookies, all of which are strictly necessary to operate the Platform. We do not use advertising, tracking, or analytics cookies.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
sessionid Maintains your logged-in session 8 hours Strictly necessary
csrftoken Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks 1 year Strictly necessary

3. No Third-Party Tracking

We do not load any third-party tracking scripts, advertising networks, or analytics platforms on the Platform. All JavaScript used by the Platform is loaded from our own servers or from content delivery networks solely for functional purposes (UI components, maps).

4. How to Control Cookies

Because we only use strictly necessary cookies, disabling them through your browser will prevent the Platform from functioning. You can manage cookies through your browser settings, but note that blocking session cookies will prevent you from logging in.

Most browsers allow you to view, delete, or block cookies via their settings menu. Refer to your browser's help documentation for instructions.

5. Changes to This Policy

If we introduce new cookies in the future, we will update this policy and notify users accordingly.

6. Contact

Questions about our use of cookies: ops@agriops.io
