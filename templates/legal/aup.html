This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") sets out the rules governing use of the AgriOps platform ("Platform") by Customers and their Authorised Users. It forms part of the Terms of Service.

By using the Platform, you agree to comply with this AUP. Violations may result in suspension or termination of access.

1. Permitted Uses

You may use the Platform to:

  • Manage your organisation's agricultural supply chain operations, including suppliers, farms, inventory, purchase orders, and sales orders;
  • Record and maintain farm-level data for EUDR and other regulatory compliance purposes;
  • Generate compliance documents, traceability certificates, and reports;
  • Collaborate with colleagues within your organisation through role-based access;
  • Access the API for integration with your own internal systems, within the rate limits of your Subscription.

2. Prohibited Uses

You must not use the Platform to:

Data integrity violations

  • Upload false, fabricated, or misleading supply chain data, farm boundaries, or compliance records;
  • Create fictitious farms, suppliers, or transactions for the purpose of generating false compliance documentation;
  • Alter records after regulatory submission in a manner intended to deceive auditors or regulators.

Unauthorised access and security

  • Attempt to access data belonging to another organisation or tenant;
  • Probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of the Platform or its infrastructure without prior written authorisation;
  • Circumvent or disable any authentication, access control, or security feature;
  • Share your login credentials with individuals outside your organisation.

System abuse

  • Transmit automated requests at a volume that unreasonably burdens the Platform (scraping, denial-of-service);
  • Introduce malware, viruses, or malicious code into the Platform;
  • Use the Platform to send unsolicited communications (spam).

Legal violations

  • Use the Platform in violation of any applicable law, including data protection, export control, anti-bribery, or agricultural regulation;
  • Process personal data in a manner inconsistent with the Data Processing Agreement and applicable data protection law;
  • Infringe the intellectual property rights of AgriOps or any third party.

Resale and misrepresentation

  • Resell, sublicense, or otherwise provide third-party access to the Platform without a separate written agreement;
  • Represent to third parties that compliance certificates generated by the Platform constitute legal guarantees of regulatory compliance (they are records of data held — regulatory liability remains with the Customer).

3. Data Quality Responsibility

The integrity of EUDR and other compliance outputs depends entirely on the accuracy of data entered. Customers are solely responsible for the completeness and accuracy of supply chain data, farm boundaries, and compliance status records uploaded to the Platform. AgriOps does not independently verify field data.

4. Reporting Violations

If you become aware of a security issue or a potential violation of this AUP, please report it promptly to security@agriops.io. We investigate all reports in good faith.

5. Enforcement

AgriOps reserves the right to:

  • Suspend or terminate access for any Customer or Authorised User found to be in violation of this AUP;
  • Remove or restrict access to specific data or features where necessary to protect the Platform or other users;
  • Report suspected illegal activity to relevant authorities.

We will use reasonable judgment in determining violations and will, where practical, provide notice and an opportunity to remedy a breach before taking action.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this AUP from time to time. Customers will be notified of material changes in accordance with the Terms of Service.

7. Contact

Questions about this AUP: legal@agriops.io
