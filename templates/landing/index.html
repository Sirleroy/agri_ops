How It Works

From farm to compliance report in three steps

1

Map your farms

Walk the farm boundary with SW Maps — export GeoJSON or CSV and upload directly. Dry-run validation catches errors before anything is saved. Every farm gets GPS coordinates, a structured farmer record, land tenure data, and a verified status — ready to attach to any batch.

2

Track procurement and sales

Log purchase orders from suppliers, receive stock into inventory, fulfil sales orders. Every transaction links back to the farms it came from — the traceability chain builds itself.

3

Generate the compliance report

When a buyer asks for proof, generate a PDF compliance certificate scoped to their order — farm coordinates, deforestation risk status, due diligence declaration. Ready in seconds.
