EUDR Ready

The EU Deforestation Regulation is in force.
Are your suppliers ready?

EUDR Regulation 2023/1115 requires operators placing soy, cocoa, coffee, palm oil, cattle, wood, and rubber on the EU market to prove their commodities did not contribute to deforestation. That proof starts at the farm boundary.

AgriOps collects, stores, and presents that proof — farm GPS coordinates, risk classifications, verification status, and compliance documents — in a format EU buyers and auditors can act on.
